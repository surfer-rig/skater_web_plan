The Future of Skate Clothes

Skaters have long since had a signature style of clothing and because of that it has been easy for skate brands to know just what type of clothes to market to the skate comunity. New skateboard brands come and go based on what niche type of graphic is popular amoungst different groups and it's usually hard for them to survive after they go out of style. With Foatch Skateboarding that will be different.

Creativity

Skaters have also long since used thier creativity in their skateboarding and in their style choices as well. Many skaters like to make their own art and dream of making their own clothes with their logos on them. With Foatch you'll be able to send your graphics to us and if we really like them you could have the chance for them to be made into real clothes that you would be able to buy or even recieve some of the profits from.

Attention Grabbers!

Skateboard brands get nowhere when they dont get noticed and so your ideas will be miuch more meaningful to us when paired with your skateboarding. Send us your clips and if we like them then we'll post them on our website. If your video gets a lot of views or likes you'll be more likely to get your ideas accepted and made into clothes! consistently send us quality content and cool relevant designs for a high chance to help get us and yourself noticed!
